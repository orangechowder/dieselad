Since 2024, AD Diesel has been the trusted name in mobile heavy-duty repair across Québec. We proudly serve a wide range of clients — from large trucking brokers and logistics firms to construction contractors and equipment operators. Depuis 2024, AD Diesel est le nom de confiance pour la réparation mobile d'équipements lourds à travers le Québec.
